## Notes

- Always prefer the current version of legislation at justice.gc.ca over any cached or third-party copy.
- Income Tax Folios are the current CRA position. Interpretation Bulletins are archived but still valid where no folio has replaced them — verify whether a folio supersedes a given IT before citing.
- CRA guides (T4001, T4032) reflect CRA's operational position but are not legally binding. They carry less weight than folios or bulletins on contested points.
- CanLII is the preferred source for case law. Cite the court, year, and CanLII citation (e.g., 2023 TCC 45, 2023 CanLII 12345).
